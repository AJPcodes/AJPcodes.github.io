I'm a newly minted software developer, finishing the Node.js MEAN-stack development track at Nashville Software School (NSS). As a naturally curious person who loves to learn and thrives in challenging environments, I can't wait to see what's next.

After obtaining my undergraduate degree in economics from Maryville College, I decided to see the world and spent four years living in Japan, while teaching English, learning Japanese, and travelling around Japan and eastern Asia. I loved my time there, especially the constant stimulation from learning new language and culture.

I've been back in the US for a little over two years now, living in Nashville and working in air cargo logistics for Nippon Express. I started part time classes at NSS in July of 2015, and learning to code has felt like discovering what I'm meant to do. The mental stimulation, the parallels to linguistics, the mix of artistic creativity and mathematical precision -- I love all of it.

The further along I get the more I realize that the tech world is as deep as it is broad. I'm dedicated to continually improving. I can't wait to dive deeper.
